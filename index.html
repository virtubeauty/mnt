Frequently Asked Questions

Which blockchain networks are supported? ▼
Mintly supports major blockchain networks including Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, and Binance Smart Chain. You can choose your preferred network during the minting process, and we optimize for the best gas fees and transaction speeds.
Can I sell my NFTs on major marketplaces? ▼
Absolutely! All NFTs created with Mintly are compatible with popular marketplaces like OpenSea, Rarible, SuperRare, and Foundation. We provide standard-compliant metadata and ensure seamless integration.
What file formats are supported? ▼
We generate high-resolution images in PNG format and provide metadata in standard JSON format. We also support animated GIFs and can export to various formats based on your collection needs.
Is there a limit on collection size? ▼
No limits! Whether you want to create a small exclusive collection of 100 pieces or a massive 10,000+ piece collection, Mintly can handle projects of any size with consistent quality and style.
How much does it cost to use Mintly? ▼
We offer flexible pricing with free demo generations to get started. Pro plans include unlimited generations, priority processing, and advanced features. Check our pricing page for detailed information.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept both cryptocurrency payments (ETH, MATIC, SOL, BNB) and traditional payment methods including credit cards and PayPal for maximum convenience.
